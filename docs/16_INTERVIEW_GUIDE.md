# Interview Guide

## Explain the Project

PrepMind AI is an AI-powered competitive exam preparation platform built using FastAPI, React, PostgreSQL, LangGraph, ChromaDB, and OpenAI.

---

## Why FastAPI?

- High Performance
- Async Support
- Automatic Swagger
- Easy Validation

---

## Why React?

- Component-based
- Reusable UI
- Fast Development

---

## Why PostgreSQL?

- Relational
- Reliable
- Scalable

---

## Why LangGraph?

- AI Workflow
- Multi-Agent System
- Extensible Architecture

---

## Why RAG?

To answer questions from uploaded PDFs instead of relying only on the LLM.

---

## Challenges Solved

- Authentication
- AI Routing
- Quiz Generation
- RAG Integration
- Interactive Quiz UI
- Dashboard Design
