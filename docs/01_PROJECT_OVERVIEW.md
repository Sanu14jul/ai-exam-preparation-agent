# PrepMind AI

> An AI-powered exam preparation platform designed to help students preparing for competitive examinations such as UPSC, SSC, Railway, Banking, BPSC, CAT, GATE, and other government or entrance exams.

---

# Project Vision

PrepMind AI aims to become an intelligent study companion that helps students prepare more efficiently using Artificial Intelligence.

Instead of relying only on static study material, students can interact with AI to generate personalized study plans, attempt AI-generated quizzes, upload PDFs for question answering, and receive instant learning assistance.

The long-term vision is to build a complete AI-powered learning ecosystem that improves productivity, personalization, and exam performance.

---

# Problem Statement

Competitive exam aspirants face several common challenges:

- Lack of personalized study plans
- Difficulty finding quality practice questions
- Large amounts of PDF study material
- Time-consuming revision
- No centralized AI-powered study assistant

PrepMind AI solves these problems through intelligent AI agents.

---

# Solution

PrepMind AI combines Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and modern web technologies to provide a complete AI learning platform.

Students can:

- Generate personalized study plans
- Create quizzes instantly
- Upload PDFs and ask questions
- Chat with an AI tutor
- Track study progress
- Review quiz performance
